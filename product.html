The YEON vita7 c-nergy vitamin cream is a skincare product that aims to provide a brightening and revitalizing effect to the skin. The cream is formulated with a potent blend of seven vitamins, including Vitamin C, E, and B5, as well as natural plant extracts like sea buckthorn and centella asiatica.The cream is designed to provide intense hydration to the skin while brightening and reducing the appearance of dark spots and hyperpigmentation. It also helps to strengthen the skin barrier and protect it from environmental stressors, leaving the skin looking and feeling revitalized.The cream has a light and non-greasy texture, making it suitable for all skin types. It is also free from parabens, sulfates, and artificial fragrances, making it a great choice for those with sensitive skin.

key benefits:

  • Brightening: The high concentration of vitamin C and other antioxidants in the cream can help brighten the skin, reduce the appearance of dark spots, and even out the skin tone.
  • Hydrating: The cream is formulated with hyaluronic acid, which is a powerful humectant that can help to deeply hydrate the skin, leaving it feeling plump and moisturized.
  • Anti-aging: The cream contains various vitamins and antioxidants that can help to fight the signs of aging by protecting the skin from environmental stressors, promoting collagen production, and reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Soothing: The cream contains Centella Asiatica extract, which is known for its soothing and healing properties. This ingredient can help to calm irritated skin, reduce redness, and promote faster healing of acne scars.
  • Nourishing: The cream also contains a blend of nourishing oils and extracts, including jojoba oil, avocado oil, and chamomile extract, which can help to provide the skin with essential nutrients and vitamins for a healthier, more radiant complexion.

key ingredients:

  • Vitamin C: Helps to brighten and even out skin tone, as well as stimulate collagen production for firmer skin.
  • Niacinamide: Improves the appearance of enlarged pores, uneven skin tone, and fine lines.
  • Adenosine: Aids in reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Panthenol: Also known as vitamin B5, it helps to soothe and hydrate the skin.
  • Betaine: A natural humectant that helps to retain moisture in the skin.
  • Centella Asiatica Extract: A soothing ingredient that helps to calm and repair the skin.
  • Portulaca Oleracea Extract: A plant extract that has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, helping to soothe and protect the skin.

how to use:

  • After cleansing and toning, take an appropriate amount of the cream and apply it evenly over your face and neck.
  • Gently massage the cream into your skin using upward and outward motions.
  • Allow the cream to absorb fully into your skin before applying any additional products.

 

It is recommended to use the cream in your morning and/or evening skincare routine.
